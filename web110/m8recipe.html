Carne de Res Guisada - Beef Stew (Dominican Style)

Ingredients Quantity
Beef 1 Pound (cubed)
Onion 1 Small (diced)
Garlic 3 Cloves (minced)
Tomato Paste 2 Tbs
Green Pepper 1 Half diced
Potatoes 3 Medium Sized
Carrots 3 Sticks (sliced)
Oregano 1 Quarter Tbs
Vegetable Oil 2 Tbs
Worcestershire Sauce 1 Tbs
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste
Beef Maggi Cube 1 Cube
Preparation
Start by heating vegetable oil in a medium sized pot.
Add the beef, and brown it for about 3 minutes.
Add in your garlic, onion, tomato paste, green pepper, oregano, maggi cube, and worcestershire sauce. Heat for another 3 min.
Add 1 cup of water, and let simmer on low heat for about 45 minutes.
After simmering for 45 minutes, add in your potatoes and carrots and simmer once again on low heat for another 30 min or until potatoes are tender.
Once potatoes are tender, the dish is ready! Best served with rice.
