Guitar Playing Hobby Site

What is the purpose of the site?

The purpose of this website is to provide a learning resource for beginner guitarists, to start them on their journey of learning the instrument.

Who is your intended audience? Be specific.

From aspiring to intermediate guitarists, any ages.

What type of content might be included in your site? Be specific.

Information regarding different types of guitars, genres, and equipment. Tutorials on how to play basic songs, chords, and tuning your guitar.

Two related websites.

Justin Guitar: Justin Guitar is a website that has many tutorials, ranging from beginner all the way to advanced. It's an excellent resource for guitar players of any skill level.

UltimateGuitar: UltimateGuitar is a website that is mainly used to post sheet music for guitarists to read, however they also have a wide range of tutorials and a forum with many helpful users.
