M4A Focus on Web Design

  • What is the company/organization?

    The company I chose is called Skatewarehouse, an online skateboarding store.

  • What is the URL?

    The URL to the website is: https://www.skatewarehouse.com

  • What is the purpose of the site?

    The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with a catalog of items to buy for everything skateboarding related. It also has clothing for sale.

  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience?

    Yes, this website does successfuly reach its audience. It has everything that a skateboarder would need, from boards to shoes and protective gear. There are many different brands that a customer can choose from to suit their needs.

  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not?

    This website was useful to me. I was easily able to navigate through the different items available for sale. The website also has a custom skateboard builder, which lets you pick different parts of the skateboard to purchase and ship to you.

  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website, etc.

    The homepage of this website features a sliding image gallery of different products for sale. To the left of the screen is a navigation bar to search for specific items. The page is neutral colors, however the images of the merchandise add much of the needed color.
